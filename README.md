Web Dev. and Designer
---------------------

Engineering student at Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai, with a specialized focus on the intersection of embedded systems, IoT architecture, and high-performance software development. My technical foundation is built on a deep understanding of microprocessor architectures (8051/ARM) and hardware-level programming, which I translate into scalable, efficiency-driven software solutions.

Beyond hardware, I am deeply engaged in web and application development, where I leverage modern frameworks to build high-performance, responsive interfaces. I enjoy the challenge of architecting custom digital ecosystems—from initial concept to full-stack deployment—ensuring that every project is both structurally sound and user-centric.

I am passionate about designing systems that prioritize structural integrity and performance—whether that means routing low-noise bio-telemetry PCBs, implementing ANN-based power system models, or architecting native mobile applications with optimized data persistence. My approach combines the rigor of hardware engineering with modern web and mobile development standards to create robust, functional tools.

Currently part of the 2027 batch, I am actively bridging academic research in embedded control and AI-ML with practical, real-world application development. My work is consistently driven by the philosophy of building at the nexus of hardware efficiency and software scalability.
